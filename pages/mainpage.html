ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are BIPOC family, a small and local owned cafe located right here in downtown Lowell, Massachusetts. The Nibbāna Café was founded in August 25, 2020 during the pandamic.

We fulfill the need for aesthetic,cozy, harmonous and safe space local environment, offering cultured gourmet coffee, tea, pastries, and breakfast.

OUR MISSION AND PURPOSE

Our mission is driven by solidarity and community. We are not only inspired to serve our community coffee and tea but our goal and purpose is also focusing in building a stronger community and support other small local businesses as well.

This means, we are commit to serve our community whom we call friends, family, and neighbors with nothing but the absolute best of our craft with transparency.

OUR COFFEE SOURCE

We source our coffee from local roasters here in Lowell from Jon and Kate, the Tiny Arms Coffee. Our roasters working directly and consciously with the farmers in different regions from all over the world to bring you the best coffee there is without compromising the livelihood of the farmers.

Solidarity and Community

The community food pantry is available everyday. We accept non-perishable food, medicine, and hygiene product donations. We also accept beanies, gloves, sock, and warmers only during fall and winter times. You are also welcome to donate money which we will use directly for the community food pantry.
